## Project Overview｜项目简介

### English

HerRhythm is a Web3 wellness product designed for women. Centered around **breathing practice, Kegel exercise, and daily on-chain check-ins**, it helps users return to their bodies, regulate their rhythm, and build a gentler relationship with health, recovery, and self-care.

It is not designed as a productivity tool that pressures users to “keep going.” Instead, it is meant to be a small, gentle window: when you feel overwhelmed, exhausted, or emotionally stretched thin, you can open it, give yourself one minute, and come back to your own breath and body.

HerRhythm is imagined as a form of **emotional companionship through wellness** — soft in tone, but meaningful in impact.

### 中文

HerRhythm 是一个面向女性的 Web3 身心健康产品，围绕 **腹式呼吸、凯格尔训练、每日链上打卡** 三个核心动作，帮助用户在忙碌、疲惫与压力之中，重新回到自己的身体，找回稳定而温柔的节律。

它不是一个“逼人坚持”的效率工具，也不想制造新的打卡压力。它更像一个给女性留出的微小窗口：当你觉得很累、很满、快要透不过气的时候，打开它，给自己一分钟，回到自己身上的一分钟。

HerRhythm 希望做成一种 **情感陪伴式的女性健康产品**：轻一点、柔一点，但真实有力。

## Background｜项目背景

### English

People are often looking outward. Unless we deliberately pause and look into a mirror, it is surprisingly easy to lose familiarity even with ourselves.

For many women, this outward orientation is even more intense. Society often expects women to care, adapt, support, nurture, organize, endure, and keep everything running — in families, relationships, workplaces, and everyday life. Women are often expected to do well in multiple roles at once: daughter, partner, mother, caregiver, employee, and more.

Under these layered expectations, many women become deeply familiar with the needs of others while gradually becoming strangers to their own bodies, stress levels, emotions, and exhaustion. We care for so much, yet often forget to care for ourselves first.

HerRhythm was created in response to this condition.

It hopes to remind women that:

- the body should not be the last thing we remember,
- self-care is not indulgence, but a basic capability,
- and returning to oneself is not retreat, but the beginning of agency.

### 中文

很多时候，人总是向外看的。  
如果不刻意照镜子，我们甚至很难真正看见自己的样子。

而女性在现实生活里，更容易长期处在一种“向外”的状态中：向外回应家庭、向外照顾关系、向外承担情绪、向外适应期待。社会常常默认女性应该温柔、体贴、懂事、奉献，要做好女儿、做好伴侣、做好母亲、做好职场角色，也要把身体、情绪、外表、秩序都打理得妥当。

在这样的多重期待之下，很多女性会慢慢变得熟悉他人的需要，却对自己的身体与情绪越来越陌生。我们关心很多事，却常常忘了先关心自己。我们会照顾别人，却不一定记得停下来照顾自己的呼吸、自己的紧绷、自己的疲惫、自己的健康。

HerRhythm 想回应的，正是这种被忽略的状态。

它希望提醒女性：

- **身体不是最后才被想起的部分**
- **自我照顾不是奢侈，而是一种基础能力**
- **回到自己，不是退缩，而是一种主体性的开始**

## Why On-chain｜为什么上链

### English

HerRhythm uses on-chain check-ins not as a decorative Web3 layer, but as a meaningful design choice.

Women’s self-care is often small, quiet, and easily overlooked. A breathing session, a Kegel exercise, a one-minute pause in the middle of exhaustion — these actions may appear minor, but they are not insignificant. They are moments of reconnection, regulation, and reclaiming attention for oneself.

Putting these rituals on-chain gives them three forms of meaning.

#### 1. To leave a trace of self-care

Many meaningful efforts disappear simply because they are never recorded. On-chain check-ins allow these gentle practices to become visible traces rather than forgotten moments.

#### 2. To make invisible care visible

Women carry immense emotional, relational, and caregiving labor, much of which remains unrecognized. HerRhythm tries to transform “taking care of oneself” from an invisible private act into something worth recording, acknowledging, and valuing.

#### 3. To connect Web3 with women’s agency

Web3 is often discussed in terms of assets, identity, or ownership. HerRhythm extends that idea into a softer but deeply personal dimension: **ownership of one’s rhythm, body awareness, and record of care.**

In this project, “leaving a mark on-chain” becomes a quiet but firm statement:

- I have the right to care for myself.
- I have the right to record that care seriously.
- I have the right to let my bodily experiences, healing, and recovery be seen, remembered, and respected.

This is why HerRhythm belongs on-chain.

### 中文

HerRhythm 选择链上记录，并不是为了给健康打卡增加一层“技术包装”，而是希望让女性那些经常被忽视的、轻柔的、日常的自我照顾，真正 **被记录、被看见、被保留**。

在现实里，女性对身体的照顾往往是碎片化的、无声的、不被放大的。可能只是一次呼吸练习，一次盆底肌训练，一次疲惫中的短暂停留。这些动作很小，但它们并不微不足道。它们是女性重新感知身体、重新确认边界、重新把注意力放回自己身上的过程。

**上链** 在 HerRhythm 里的意义，主要有三层：

#### 1. 让女性对自己的照顾留下痕迹

很多努力之所以容易被忽略，是因为它们没有被记录。链上打卡让这些看似微小的练习，不再只是转瞬即逝的日常，而是成为可以被保留的行动痕迹。

#### 2. 让“自我照顾”从隐形劳动变成被看见的价值

女性长期承担着大量情绪劳动、照顾劳动和关系劳动，但这些付出常常并不被认真衡量。HerRhythm 希望把“照顾自己”这件事，从一个容易被忽略的私人动作，转化成一种值得被记录和被肯定的价值表达。

#### 3. 让链上记录与女性主体性发生连接

Web3 的意义不只是拥有资产、身份和交易，也可以是：**一个人如何拥有自己的节律、自己的身体感受、自己的记录权。**

HerRhythm 试图把“链上留名”从技术行为，变成一种更温柔也更坚定的表达：

- **我有权照顾自己**
- **我有权把这份照顾认真留下**
- **我有权让自己的身体经验和恢复过程，被看见、被承认、被记住**

这也是 HerRhythm 选择上链的核心原因。不是为了增加压力，而是为了让女性把属于自己的那一部分，郑重地写回自己身上。

## Experience Logic｜产品体验逻辑

### English

HerRhythm emphasizes coherence in emotional tone and interface experience:

- the home page builds a calm, breathable atmosphere
- practice pages emphasize rhythm and immersion
- completion states focus on encouragement rather than pressure
- the check-in page focuses on witness and record rather than anxiety

A key product decision is that:

- the contract still allows **only one on-chain check-in per day**
- breathing and Kegel can still be completed independently
- if a user has already checked in on-chain that day, completing another session is **not framed as wasted effort**
- instead, the product gently says:  
  **“This session is complete. Today’s on-chain check-in has already been recorded.”**

This is important because the practice itself already matters, even when it does not create a second on-chain record.

### 中文

HerRhythm 的设计重点不是“功能堆砌”，而是体验统一：

- 首页建立温柔、留白、可呼吸的氛围
- 训练页强调节律感与沉浸感
- 完成态强调被鼓励，而不是被催促
- 打卡页强调记录与见证，而不是制造焦虑

尤其在链上打卡逻辑上，HerRhythm 做了一个明确选择：

- **合约保持每天最多一次链上打卡**
- 呼吸和凯格尔训练本身可以分别完成
- 如果当天已经链上打卡，再完成另一项训练时，不会被表达成“白做了”
- 而是温柔提示：  
  **本次训练已完成，今日链上打卡已完成，无需重复记录**

我希望产品传达的是：训练本身就是对身体有意义的，不必一切都被“次数化”才算数。
